About Regen Creatives

Regen Creatives builds business systems for founders who need structure before scale.

Regen Creatives is a product and systems studio focused on helping small businesses operate more clearly, more consistently, and with less manual strain. We design practical tools, workflows, and AI-supported systems that reduce friction in day-to-day operations so founders can stop relying on memory, patchwork, and repeated effort.

The goal is not to add noise in the form of more dashboards, more automation, or more moving parts. The goal is to build a business that runs with greater clarity, stronger follow-through, and better use of time.

Our Mission

Regen Creatives exists to help founders build operational structure that supports growth. We believe automation is only useful when the underlying workflow is clear. Our work begins with understanding how a business actually runs, where the breakdowns are, and what should be standardized before more tools are introduced.

  • Clarify how the business currently operates
  • Identify repeated friction, delays, and workflow gaps
  • Design systems that are usable, maintainable, and worth keeping
What We Build

We build tools and system-led products that help founders handle visibility, lead flow, content operations, task execution, and customer communication with more consistency and less waste.

  • AutoSite AI: systems-led website automation for service businesses
  • Fix Pixie: guided problem-solving for repair and DIY workflows
  • REVA AI: lead qualification and follow-up support for real estate businesses
  • ViewerMatch and related tools: products that support discovery, visibility, and business operations

Founder

Lorraine Dukes is the founder of Regen Creatives. She works at the intersection of product design, systems thinking, and applied AI, helping founders move from scattered effort to more structured business operations.

Her background includes more than a decade of experience in design, user experience, and digital product work. That experience shapes the way Regen Creatives approaches automation: not as a novelty, but as an extension of clear process design, useful tools, and sound operational decisions.

Lorraine’s work is especially focused on the realities of small business. Many founders do not need a complicated technology stack. They need a clearer way to handle leads, follow-up, task flow, documentation, and repeatable work. Regen Creatives is built around solving that problem in a practical way.

Based in Georgia, Lorraine develops products and frameworks that help businesses reduce friction, strengthen consistency, and build systems that can grow without becoming harder to manage.

How We Work

Regen Creatives approaches growth through structure first. We look at how work enters the business, how it moves, where it stalls, and what should be documented, simplified, or supported with automation. The result is a more dependable operating system for the business, not just another layer of software.
