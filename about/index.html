About Regen Creatives

Regen Creatives is a product studio dedicated to building AI-powered tools and automation systems that help solopreneurs and small operators solve real problems and reduce repetitive work. The company’s ethos centers on practical, reliable solutions rather than hype, enabling businesses to work more efficiently in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Our Mission

At Regen Creatives, we believe that AI should be accessible, practical, and empowering. Our mission is to build tools that simplify complex workflows, boost productivity, and unlock new opportunities for creators and small teams.

  • Deliver tools that automate repeatable work
  • Focus on usability and clarity
  • Enable users to launch solutions quickly
What We Build

Our suite of tools harnesses AI to help creators run operations, optimize content, and build digital assets without deep technical overhead.

  • ChannelBooster: YouTube optimization and growth analytics.
  • AutoSite AI: Rapid AI-powered website generator.
  • Fix Pixie: Smart visual problem-solving assistant.
  • ViewMatch, REVA AI, Glam Book AI: Tools in development that expand discovery, lead qualification, and business automation.

Founder and CEO

Lorraine Dukes is the founder and AI Product Strategist behind Regen Creatives. She leads the company’s vision of building practical, automation-driven tools that help solopreneurs and small teams cut through overhead and focus on meaningful work. Lorraine combines systems thinking with real-world product experience to design workflows that solve persistent operational challenges.

Before founding Regen Creatives, Lorraine transitioned from a corporate design and strategy background into AI product build-outs, emphasizing accessibility and human-centered automation. She shares insights on career pivots into AI and encourages builders to pair practical systems with automation for everyday business leverage.

Lorraine blends product strategy with design systems expertise and more than a decade of professional experience. She heads the development of purpose-built SaaS products that help founders automate daily tasks and scale without unnecessary complexity.

Based in Georgia, Lorraine focuses on turning creative problem-solving into repeatable systems. Her approach reflects a belief that automation should make work simpler, not more complicated. This freeing up time for creators to iterate, grow, and focus on what matters most.
