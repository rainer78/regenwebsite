Approximately 5 minutes. There are 20 questions, each requiring a simple selection from three options.

What do the tier classifications mean?

Your tier reflects your current AI readiness: AI Exposed (0-25%), AI Curious (26-50%), AI Enabled (51-75%), or AI Integrated (76-100%). Each tier comes with specific recommendations.

What is included in the Pro upgrade?

The Pro upgrade includes a detailed 90-Day AI Advantage Plan, department-specific automation opportunities, an ROI tracking template, and a competitive risk exposure analysis.

Yes. Your assessment responses and email are stored securely. We do not share your data with third parties.
