How the RainFlix Library System Works

Emby handles media

Movies, shows, and ripped CD audio stay in Emby, where the streaming and media management belong.

RainFlix handles experience

RainFlix becomes the branded interface with shelves, featured rows, curated categories, and cleaner presentation.

Games stay separate

Your games should live as browser-based projects with their own URLs. RainFlix simply catalogs and launches them.

JSON ties it together

One feed can represent Emby media data, and another can represent your games. RainFlix reads both and renders the front page.
