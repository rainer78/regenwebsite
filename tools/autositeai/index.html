Common Questions

Everything you need to know before putting your website on autopilot.

How do I update my website with AutoSite AI?
Unlike WordPress or Webflow, you don't. You simply post to your social channels or close a project. Our AI detects the activity, rewrites it into professional copy, and updates your site for you automatically.
How is this different from Wix, Squarespace, or Shopify?
Platforms like Wix and Shopify only sell you a builder—a static tool that requires you to do all the work. Most of these sites go stale within months because business owners are too busy to edit them.

AutoSite AI is different: it’s an active engine. While Google rewards "Freshness," most manual sites lose rankings over time. AutoSite AI ensures your site stays updated daily with your latest activity, signaling to search engines that you are an active, authoritative business without you ever lifting a finger.
Can I use this with my existing WordPress, Wix, or Shopify site?
Absolutely. We offer two paths: We can fully migrate your site to our high-performance autonomous platform, OR we can integrate AutoSite AI directly into your existing WordPress, Wix, or Shopify setup. We take your current content and "upgrade" it with our autonomous engine so your site starts updating itself without you ever having to touch a web editor again.
Does this replace my Web Designer?
No. Think of AutoSite AI as your **Website Manager**, not your designer. Your designer builds the house; AutoSite AI makes sure the lights are on and the front yard is clean by keeping the content fresh and relevant.
Do I need to be a "tech person" to set this up?
Not at all. We built AutoSite AI specifically for operators, not developers.

Our team handles the initial "Bridge Connection" to your existing WordPress, Wix, or Shopify site. Once that one-time link is established, your website begins listening to your business activity automatically. No coding, no manual maintenance, and no technical headaches on your end—ever.
How does an "Autonomous" site actually grow my business?
In 2026, a static website is a "dead" website. When a potential customer lands on your page and sees a portfolio project or a client win from this morning, it creates instant authority.

Most business owners lose leads because their site looks like a "ghost town." AutoSite AI ensures your digital storefront always reflects your real-world hustle. By showing prospects that you are active and available right now, you build the immediate trust required to turn a casual visitor into a high-value client—all without you ever having to "sit down and blog."
Will I still have total control over what is published?
100%. We know your reputation is your most valuable asset. While the system is designed to save you time, it never goes "rogue."

You can choose your level of involvement:
  • Full Autopilot: For those who want hands-free growth.
  • Review First: You receive a quick mobile notification or email to "Approve" or "Edit" a post before it ever goes live.

Think of the AI as a highly-trained junior copywriter—it does the heavy lifting, but you always have the final say on the "Publish" button.
